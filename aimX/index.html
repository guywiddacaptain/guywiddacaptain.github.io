AimX CG - Crosshair Generator by
The AimX Crosshair Generator is a browser-based crosshair generator that allows for the making of a variety of crosshairs without the hassle of using external tools like Photoshop or GIMP.

Use the inputs in combinations to create your own unique crosshairs, or check out the presets provided for some pre-made crosshairs!
